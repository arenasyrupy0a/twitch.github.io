Frequently Asked Questions

What can i get?

First you need to decide what exactly you want to get. We provide the opportunity to choose a free item for your inventory, add an e-sportsman to your friends list, or receive a comment from an e-sportsman.

How to get skins?

To receive a free skin in your inventory, you need to
1. Scroll to the bottom of the site, then click the "Get skins" button.
2. Select the skin you want to get.
3. Then click on the “Get your Prize” button.

To receive a comment from e-sportsman, you need to

How long does it take to receive the skins after linking my Steam account?

Due to a large number of requests, it takes exactly 48 hours from the moment you connect your Steam account to the time you receive the skins. Please be patient while we process your request.

Is Twitch Prime required to receive skins?

No, Twitch Prime is not required to receive skins during this event. Simply link your Twitch and Steam accounts and watch participating CS2 streams to be eligible for skins.

Why are we giving out prizes?

We're giving out prizes to create an engaging and rewarding experience for viewers, promote the CS2 category on Twitch, and support our partners. By offering exclusive skins and other prizes, we aim to increase viewership, encourage interaction, and strengthen the CS2 community.

How do we find your account on Steam?

To find your Steam account, we rely on the account linking process between Twitch and Steam. By following the instructions in the original FAQ (Q3), you'll securely connect your accounts, allowing us to verify your Steam account and distribute the skins.

Which e-sportsman will add me to their friends list?

From which e-sportsman stream you came to this site, that player will add you to his friends list.
